Physics Note:
 Lens Equation: μA/u + μB/v = (μL − μA)/R1 - (μL − μB)/R2

Surface Powers:
Φ1 = (μL − μA) / R1
Φ2 = (μL − μB) / R2

Total Power (Thin Lens):
Φ = Φ1 - Φ2

Generalized Lens Equation:
μA / u + μB / v = Φ

Image Distance:
v = μB / ( Φ - μA / u )

Focal Lengths:
fA = μA / Φ (object-side focal length)
fB = μB / Φ (image-side focal length)

Magnification:
m = (μA / μB) · (v / u)
himg = m · hobj
